Transforming Realism into Impressionism

Combining photography and oil pastel, these handcrafted works of art are made to order.

  • Choose from a variety of photographs by the artist, or upload your own image.
  • Square format, multiple dimensions and finishes available.
  • Custom orders available
  • Each image is printed on archival paper and embellished with oil pastel, then cut to size and mounted with archival adhesive to a square panel.
  • Finished with several layers of protective coating.
